## Agile Delivery Framework: Integrated Data & Brands

> **A Hybrid-Agile approach to Data Engineering & Analytics.**

### Achieving Consistent Value Delivery through Multi-Disciplinary Sprints

## Strategic Vision
To establish a high-transparency, data-driven workflow that synchronizes **Data Engineering**, **Software Development**, and **Brand Marketing**. This framework ensures we move from raw data to a shippable, predictive product increment every 14 days, maintaining a "Retail 2.0" competitive edge.

---

## Integrated Workflow & Sync
To achieve a "Shippable Product Increment," we break down silos between technical and commercial teams using a unified ClickUp ecosystem.



### 1. The Multi-Disciplinary Sprint
* **Data Engineers:** Maintain Delta Lake health and execute model retraining.
* **Dev Team:** Build high-performance API endpoints and Dashboard UI for brand partners.
* **QA:** Perform automated validation of code stability and AI model precision.
* **Marketing:** Execute growth experiments using PBI insights to prove ROI to brand partners.

### 2. The Definition of Done (DoD)
A product increment is only "Shippable" if it meets the following cross-functional criteria:
1.  **Technical:** Code passes 85% unit test coverage and peer review in GitHub.
2.  **AI:** Random Forest model achieves >90% precision on the validation set.
3.  **Marketing:** Documentation and "Brand Success Kits" are updated for the new feature.

---

##  Measuring Value & Transparency via ClickUp
We use **Empirical Process Control** (Transparency, Inspection, Adaptation) to ensure we are building the *right* product for the Shopping ecosystem.

* **Value Evaluation:** We track "Predictive Lift"—the percentage increase in brand conversion rates driven by our AI vs. the control group.
* **Transparency:** All work is visualized in **ClickUp Dashboards**, where bottlenecks (e.g., Data cleaning delays) are flagged via custom statuses.
* **Sprint Review:** A live demonstration where Brand Stakeholders provide direct feedback on the new PBI functionality.

---

## 4 Levels of Agile Maturity
We evaluate our team's growth using this 4-level framework to move toward high-performance delivery.



| Level | Maturity Stage | Characteristics | Key Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **L1** | **Reactive** | Sprints are disorganized; Data and Dev teams work in silos. | Throughput |
| **L2** | **Stable** | Standard Scrum ceremonies (Daily Standup, Review) are consistent in ClickUp. | Velocity |
| **L3** | **Integrated** | Data, Dev, and Marketing are fully synced; DoD is strictly followed. | Say/Do Ratio |
| **L4** | **Value-Driven** | Team self-optimizes; AI models retrain based on ROI metrics automatically. | Predictive Lift |

## ClickUp Tooling & Reporting Stack
To maintain a single source of truth, we utilize the following ClickUp features:

* **Sprint Lists:** Managing the unified backlog for Dev, Data, and Marketing.
* **Gantt View:** Visualizing high-level dependencies between Data Engineering and Marketing launches.
* **ClickUp Whiteboards:** Used for collaborative Sprint Planning and Retrospectives.
* **Dashboard Reports:** Real-time tracking of Velocity, Lead Time, and Cumulative Flow.
* **ClickUp Docs:** Centralized knowledge base replacing Confluence for strategy wikis and SOPs.
