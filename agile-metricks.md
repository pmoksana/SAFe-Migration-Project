##  Metrics Philosophy
For the **Shopping PBI** project, we utilize an "Evidence-Based Management" (EBM) approach. We don't just track how fast the team moves; we track the **Value** delivered by the AI and the **Stability** of the platform. Our metrics are divided into three critical tiers: **Process**, **Quality**, and **AI Intelligence**.

---

##  1. Process Metrics (The Delivery Engine)
These metrics measure the efficiency of our Scrum framework and the predictability of our releases.

### Velocity & Sprint Health
* **Average Velocity:** 48 Story Points (Calculated over a 3-Sprint rolling average).
* **Say/Do Ratio:** **94%**. This high ratio proves our team’s maturity in estimation and planning accuracy.
* **Cycle Time:** **3.2 Days**. The average time an item takes to move from "In Progress" to "Done."



| Metric | Target | Current Performance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sprint Burndown** | Linear Trend | Consistent (No late-sprint spikes) |
| **Lead Time** | < 10 Days | 8.5 Days (From Backlog to Production) |
| **Spillover Rate** | < 10% | 6% (Minimal carry-over to next Sprint) |

---

##  2. AI & Product Metrics (The PBI Core)
As a Predictive Brand Intelligence tool, the "Definition of Done" includes the mathematical accuracy of our Random Forest Multi-label Classifiers.

### AI Precision Matrix
| Metric | Benchmark | Result | Business Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Precision** | > 85% | **91%** | Minimizes "False Positives" in brand targeting. |
| **Recall** | > 80% | **87%** | Captures the majority of high-intent shoppers. |
| **F1-Score** | 0.82 | **0.89** | Optimal balance between reach and accuracy. |



### Consumer Impact Metrics
* **Predictive Lift:** **+31%** increase in conversion compared to non-AI segments.
* **Recommendation Relevancy:** Measured by a **42% Click-Through Rate (CTR)** on "Next-Best-Action" triggers.
* **Data Refresh Rate:** AI models are retrained every **24 hours** to account for shifting consumer trends.

---

##  3. Quality & Stability Metrics (Technical Debt)
We ensure that high velocity does not lead to a fragile codebase.

* **Code Coverage:** **88%** (Automated Unit & Integration tests).
* **Escaped Defects:** **1.2 per month** (Critical issues found in Production).
* **System Latency:** **185ms** (95th percentile for predictive API calls).
* **MTTR (Mean Time to Recovery):** **< 45 Minutes** for critical P1 incidents.
